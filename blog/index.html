AI for Cybersecurity: Predicting & Preventing Threats

With cyber threats growing in complexity, traditional security measures are no longer enough. AI-driven cybersecurity leverages machine learning, predictive analytics, and automation to detect and neutralize cyber threats before they escalate.

Real-Time Threat Detection: AI analyzes network traffic and detects anomalies, identifying cyberattacks before they cause damage.

Adaptive Defense Mechanisms: Unlike rule-based systems, AI evolves with emerging threats, adapting to new malware, ransomware, and phishing tactics.

Automated Incident Response: AI-powered security systems isolate threats, contain breaches, and suggest countermeasures in real-time, reducing human intervention and response time.

Behavioral Analytics: AI tracks user behavior to flag suspicious activities like unauthorized access or insider threats.
