Riskie- ERM Scorecard

  • Quantifies risk exposure across strategic objectives using a weighted, data-driven approach
  • Combines risk register, impact ratings, and leading indicators for a dynamic risk profile
  • Enables management and the board to prioritize and monitor key risks in real time

The ERM Scorecard is a predictive tool that visually links your organization’s strategic objectives to specific risk elements. It multiplies the importance of each objective, the impact of each risk, and relevant performance indicators to generate actionable risk scores. This helps leadership focus on the most critical risks, supporting better-informed, proactive decisions.
