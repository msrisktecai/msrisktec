1. RBI ITGRCA Assurance & Implementation

We provide specialized services to address the stringent requirements of the RBI Directions on IT Governance, Risk, Controls, and Assurance (ITGRCA), 2023. Our solutions ensure your financial institution's technology framework meets the regulator's highest standards for resilience and security.

Integrated Risk Management Develop a centralized, continuous IT risk identification and monitoring program. Central Risk Register + Mitigation Program

2. DPDP Act (2023) Readiness & Implementation

The Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023, introduces mandatory obligations for every organization processing digital personal data. Our solution provides the technical and procedural foundation to meet every statutory requirement.

Focus Area Compliance Challenge MS RiskTec Solution

Statutory Requirements New policies, ROPA, breach notifications. Policy Redesign + ROPA Creation
Technical Compliance Ensuring security controls align with DPDP. System Gap Analysis + Remediation
