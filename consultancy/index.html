How MS Risktec Consulting Is Different

Our 3-Stage Consulting Methodology

At MS Risktec, we apply a proprietary consulting methodology that transforms complex regulations into structured, auditable, and business-ready processes.

Stage 1: Foundational Governance (Assessment)
We conduct a comprehensive gap analysis and map your data and AI landscape. This stage defines the “Rules of Engagement” and establishes foundational policies such as the DPDP framework or AI risk strategy.

Stage 2: Process & Operational Alignment (Execution)
Compliance is operationalized by integrating the RBO App into daily workflows, including consent management, data principal rights logging, and third-party risk vetting to ensure continuous adherence.

Stage 3: Technical Resilience & Assurance (Audit)
We perform technical verification, breach simulation drills, and independent audits, concluding with the issuance of the MS Risktec Letter of Conformity (LoC) to demonstrate regulatory readiness.
