Our AI Solutions Suite

1. AIMS Governance Solution (ISO 42001 + BIS AI)

Unify Global & National Standards: Establish a robust AI Management System (AIMS) using the internationally recognized framework of ISO/IEC 42001, seamlessly integrated with the national requirements of BIS AI (IS 18265).

Key Deliverables:

  • AIMS Policy Frameworks: Define ethics, accountability, oversight, and control.
  • AI Risk Registers: Comprehensive identification and tracking aligned with NIST RMF.
  • DPDP Compliance Modules: Adherence to DPDP 7 Sutras.
  • Model Cards: Purpose, data lineage, and performance documentation.

2. AI Audit & Compliance Automation (BIS AI DPDP Audit App)

Powered by the BIS AI & DPDP Compliance Scoring Application: Automates assessment, scoring, and assurance with real-time compliance maturity evaluation.

Key Assessment Areas:

  • DPDP Compliance (7 Sutras): DPO, consent, encryption, retention, cross-border, breach response.
  • AI Risk Assessment (BIS): Algorithmic, Data, Operational, Ethical Risk.
  • AI Governance Assessment: AI Charter, policies, and integrated RMS.

3. AI Risk Intelligence Suite

AI-powered tools providing quantitative and real-time risk assessment, feeding insights back into AIMS for proactive governance.

  • Riskie – ERM quantitative risk scoring
  • CyberSure – AI Cyber Risk Monitor
  • Sentra – Reputational Risk Scanner
  • Rinnie – AI Risk Assessor
